## 🎯 How It Works

### 1. Open AI Sakhi
- Click "AI Sakhi Assistant" in sidebar
- Click "Chat with AI Sakhi (Titan)" button

### 2. Start Chatting
- Type your message
- Press Enter or click send button
- AI responds with context from conversation

### 3. Continuous Conversation
- Each message remembers previous context
- Have natural back-and-forth conversations
- AI understands follow-up questions

**You**: "I need help with payment"
